About Us

OralVigil Foundation is dedicated to spreading awareness about oral cancer and promoting early detection and prevention. We organize campaigns, workshops, and free check-ups to educate the public about oral health.

Our Campaigns

Free Oral Check-ups

Providing free oral cancer screenings to communities in need.

Awareness Workshops

Educational sessions on oral health and early detection techniques.

School Programs

Teaching children about oral hygiene and prevention practices.
