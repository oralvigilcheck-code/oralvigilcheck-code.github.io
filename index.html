About Us

OralVigil Foundation is a non-profit initiative dedicated to spreading awareness about oral cancer, encouraging early screening, and empowering communities with accurate, evidence-based dental information.

Our Mission

  • Promote early detection of oral cancer
  • Educate people about risk factors and symptoms
  • Conduct community screening camps
  • Support patients through guidance and resources
