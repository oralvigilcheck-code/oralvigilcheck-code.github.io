About Us

OralVigil Foundation is a dental-led non-profit organization dedicated to reducing the burden of oral cancer through awareness, education, and early screening. Our goal is to empower communities with life-saving knowledge and access to preventive care.

Our Mission

To ensure every individual recognizes early signs of oral cancer and seeks timely care, ultimately reducing preventable deaths.

  • Spreading awareness about early symptoms
  • Organizing dental camps & oral cancer screenings
  • Promoting tobacco & alcohol cessation
  • Educating on lifestyle risk factors
  • Supporting patients & families with guidance
