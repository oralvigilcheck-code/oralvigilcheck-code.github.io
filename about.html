About Us

Guarding Every Smile.

Who We Are

OralVigil Foundation is a dental-led non-profit organization focused on oral cancer awareness, early detection, and community education. We believe that awareness and timely action can save countless lives.

Why We Started

Oral cancer cases are rising globally, yet most people remain unaware of early symptoms. Our foundation was created to bridge this gap by providing accurate knowledge, accessible screening drives, and community-focused prevention programs.

What We Do

Awareness

Campaigns, workshops, school programs, and social media education.

Early Detection

Screening camps, dental checkups, and risk factor assessment programs.

Community Support

Helping families understand, prevent, and manage oral cancer risks.

Core Values

  • Compassion
  • Integrity
  • Community First
  • Medical Excellence
  • Transparency

Vision for the Future

To create a world where oral cancer is detected early, prevented widely, and understood universally. Our goal is to expand screenings, strengthen communities, and make oral cancer awareness a household concept.
