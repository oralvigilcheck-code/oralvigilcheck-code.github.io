About Us – OralVigil Foundation

OralVigil Foundation is a non-profit initiative dedicated to creating awareness, providing early detection support, and promoting preventive care for oral cancer. Our mission is to ensure that every individual—regardless of background—has access to the right information, timely screening, and compassionate guidance.

Our Vision

A future where every person is informed, empowered, and protected from preventable oral diseases—especially oral cancer.

Our Mission

  • Spread scientifically accurate, easy-to-understand information on oral cancer
  • Encourage early self-screening and professional checkups
  • Support communities with awareness drives and workshops
  • Collaborate with healthcare professionals and volunteers
  • Promote a tobacco-free, cancer-aware society

What We Do

  • Awareness campaigns
  • Early detection guidance
  • Educational resources
  • Community outreach
  • Patient support information
